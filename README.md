# Safety, Health, and Insurance Module

## Overview
This module provides comprehensive safety, health, and insurance management features. It helps organizations manage workplace safety, incident reporting, employee safety feedback, fire safety, accident investigations, vehicle inspections, and insurance documentation.

## Features

### Safety Management
- **Office Inspection Management**
  - Schedule and record office safety inspections
  - Assign safety officers
  - Generate inspection checklists
  - Track compliance and follow-up tasks

- **Incident Reporting System**
  - Report incidents with detailed information
  - Attach supporting documents
  - Categorize incidents
  - Track resolution status

- **Fire Extinguisher Records**
  - Maintain fire extinguisher database
  - Track location and maintenance history
  - Set inspection reminders
  - Log maintenance activities

### Vehicle Safety
- **Vehicle Safety Inspection**
  - Schedule inspections
  - Comprehensive inspection checklists
  - Track maintenance tasks
  - Generate compliance reports

### Insurance Management
- **Insurance Claims**
  - Process insurance claims
  - Multiple insurance types support
  - Document management
  - Claim status tracking

- **Insurance Types**
  - Property Insurance
  - Commercial Insurance
  - Third-Party Insurance
  - Inland Insurance
  - Employee Insurance

### Employee Safety
- **Employee Suggestion System**
  - Submit safety suggestions
  - Anonymous submission option
  - Track implementation status
  - Manage feedback workflow

### Investigation Management
- **Accident Investigation**
  - Record investigation details
  - Assign investigation teams
  - Root cause analysis
  - Corrective action tracking

## Installation

1. Copy the module to your addons directory
2. Update the module list
3. Install the module from the Apps menu
4. Configure the module settings in the Safety & Insurance Configuration menu

## Configuration

1. Go to Safety & Insurance > Configuration > Settings
2. Configure the following options:
   - Automatic inspection reminders
   - Safety dashboard
   - Insurance claim automation
   - Insurance analytics
   - Vehicle maintenance reminders
   - Safety training management

## Usage

### Safety Management
1. Navigate to Safety Management > Office Inspections to create and manage inspections
2. Use Incident Reports to record and track safety incidents
3. Manage fire extinguisher records in the Fire Extinguishers menu

### Vehicle Safety
1. Access Vehicle Safety > Vehicle Inspections to manage vehicle safety checks
2. Schedule and track vehicle maintenance tasks

### Insurance Management
1. Create and manage insurance claims in Insurance Management > Insurance Claims
2. Configure insurance types and policies in Insurance Types

### Employee Safety
1. Access Employee Safety > Safety Suggestions to manage employee feedback
2. Review and implement safety suggestions

### Investigations
1. Conduct accident investigations through Investigations > Accident Investigations
2. Track investigation progress and outcomes

## Security

The module includes two security groups:
- Insurance User: Basic access to create and edit records
- Insurance Manager: Full access to all features and configuration
